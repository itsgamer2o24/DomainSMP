The Royal Decree

Laws of the Land

✦
  • I

    Respect All Players

    Treat every member of the realm with dignity. Harassment, slurs, and toxic behaviour will not be tolerated.

  • II

    No Griefing or Theft

    Destroying others' builds or stealing from unclaimed areas is forbidden. Honour among lords is expected.

  • III

    No Cheating or Hacking

    Hacked clients, exploits, and unfair advantages are banned outright. Skill defines the true monarch.

  • IV

    No Spam or Advertising

    Keep chat clean and purposeful. Do not advertise other servers or flood the kingdom's channels.

  • V

    Obey the Staff

    Decisions made by staff are final. Disputes should be raised respectfully, not contested publicly.

  • VI

    Keep It Appropriate

    This is a community for all ages. Inappropriate builds, names, or discussions are not permitted.
